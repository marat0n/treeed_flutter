## 0.1.1

* Add loadables:
    - `LoadableValue` union that built with a dart's OOP functionality. It's variants are the `Got`, `Loading` and `Failure`;
    - Bindings by `.bind` and `.valueOr` methods, also matchable with a switch expression;
    - Extension methods for `TreeedState` for short creating a loadable value container: `.tsLoadableFailable` and `.tsLoadable`;
* Minor fixes;
* Add example (in `/example` directory);
* Add extension for creating `TreeedObservable` easily by `.observable` method.
